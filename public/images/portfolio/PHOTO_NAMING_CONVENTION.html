Photo Naming Convention

Name files in lowercase with hyphens (no spaces). Include a numeric suffix at the end; starting at 001, 002, …, 009, 010, 011, …, 099, 100, 101, …, 998, 999.

Example
wedding-smith-ceremony-001.jpg wedding-smith-ceremony-002.jpg family-johnson-outdoor-001.jpg headshot-sarah-corporate-001.jpg

Why this way specifically?

The alt text will auto-generate by converting hyphens to spaces and title-casing the words (the numeric suffix is stripped).

Example
wedding-smith-ceremony-001.jpg → alt: "Wedding Smith Ceremony" action-rock-climbing-001.jpg → alt: "Action Rock Climbing"

How to Name Your Files

Format: category-subject-type-NUMBER.jpg
  • All lowercase
  • Use hyphens between words, NO SPACES
  • End with a 3-digit number: 001, 002, 003, …
  • File type: .jpg, .png, .webp, or .avif
